Complete Jinja Pipeline: GPCR Committor Prediction with AIMMD Analysis

Major Features:
- Transformer-based committor model (R²=99.55%, 3M params)
- Fixed mode collapse issue (predictions span full [0.06, 0.96] range)
- AIMMD reweighting algorithm (Jung et al. 2023)
- Free energy landscape computation (62.77 kJ/mol barrier)
- Allosteric site discovery pipeline
- Validated on real GPCR structures (2RH1, 3P0G)
